About Us

Hi9's' a rapidly growing tech startup based in beautiful Cornwall.

Founded in 2014, our company mission was simple; to provide affordable computers directly or via an organisation to those that needed them the most whether on low income, disabled, vulnerable or socially excluded.

We're proud to say our ideas earned us a place on the Google Launchpad programme which has provided us with a huge amount of help, advice and support in development.

But it wasn't long into the project that we discovered another challenge - to make the web itself simple to use. Our ideas? To make organising websites easy but most importantly easy to share with friends, family or colleuges. This was when deko was first dreamt up.
